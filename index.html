Members and Missionaries

Bro. Perez (sitting in front right), Sis. Perez (wearing eyeglasses)

For hundred of years people of Ormoc were sleeping and learned to wake up on year 1970 when the full-time missionaries open a group of people called Mormon Member. They brought to us glad tidings in our lives.

SAINTS TAKE A SMILE AFTER SUNDAY SERVICE

Cogon Meeting house (Devaras), March 25, 1979

The saints rented many different kinds of an apartment just to have a meeting house to observe the sabbath day holy. Some meeting houses before has no picture, yet places were still in memories of the saints where they held Sunday Services.

District President Jet Benedict Lao and his wife cut the ribbon

July 4, 1985

Because of the faith of the members and for obeying the law of tithing the saints were blessed with a wonderful building where the members can worship God spiritually. In the year 1984, the chapel was constructed and groundbreaking was done. The chapel was finished on the year 1985 and was dedicated on the 4th of July 1985, were President Jet Benedict Lao and his wife cut the ribbon during the open house of the chapel.

Brother Florante Ablen (center)

First returned missionary of Ormoc Philippines District, March 25, 1979

The district was blessed with Returned Missionaries who helped the different branches grow. The first "RM" was Brother Florante "TIKOL" Ablen, He serves as Branch President in Branch 1 before, and was called and sustained as District Missionary.
